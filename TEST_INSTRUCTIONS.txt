NOTIFICATION SYSTEM:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Click Features:
- Click notification bell to open/close dropdown
- Dropdown shows 10 tailored notifications per user role
- Each notification has: icon, title, description, timestamp
- Unread notifications highlighted in light blue
- Badge shows unread count
- Auto-close when clicking outside

Role-Specific Notifications:
- Patient: Sessions, progress, medication, exercises, milestones
- Doctor: Patient alerts, assignments, lab results, meetings
- Physio: Sessions, exercise plans, equipment, assessments

Format:
- Title: Bold, single line with ellipsis if long
- Description: 2 lines max with ellipsis (...)
- Icons: Info (ℹ️), Success (✅), Warning (⚠️)
- Time: Relative timestamps (5 min ago, Yesterday, etc.)
